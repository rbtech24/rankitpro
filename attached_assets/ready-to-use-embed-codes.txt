⚙️ CUSTOMIZATION OPTIONS
-------------------------
Replace these values in the URLs above:

• your-domain.com → Your actual Rank It Pro domain
• marketing-test-company → Your company slug or ID number
• type=testimonials → testimonials, blogs, reviews, or all
• limit=5 → Number of items to show (1-50)
