This is the most flexible method. Just paste this single line of code anywhere in your HTML:

Customization Options:
  • Company: Replace marketing-test-company with your company slug or ID
  • Content Type: testimonials, blogs, reviews, or all
  • Limit: Number of items to display (1-50)
